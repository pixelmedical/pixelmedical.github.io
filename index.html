About Us

Pixel Medical Inc. is dedicated to providing high quality software development support to research groups and medical device manufacturers. We have extensive experience using state of the art open source components to reduce development time and cost for our clients.

Our core team are expert developers of the 3D Slicer software, a world leading open-source initiative in medical image computing. By leveraging the power in Slicer's core libraries we can develop complete medical prototypes and products in less time, and at less cost than our competitors.

And Slicer is liberally licensed under BSD, so you have complete freedom to sell your product - sans pesky royalty fees.

We have experience in many diverse areas of medical image computing.

  • Radiotherapy
  • Image guided therapy
  • Virtual & augmented reality
  • Hardware & device support
  • Machine learning & artificial intelligence

We believe the future of medical software lies in leveraging existing proven tooling to build innovative ideas into world leading applications.

Our Services

System Development

Validation
